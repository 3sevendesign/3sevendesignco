What you receive

Every piece your brand
needs to show up right.

A brand identity isn't just a logo. It's a complete visual system that tells people who you are before you say a word. Here's everything included in every project.

01

Primary Logo

Your main mark — designed to work at any size, in any context. Built with intent, not just aesthetics. You'll receive full-color, reversed, and single-color versions in every file format you need.

02

Logo Variations

A secondary mark, icon/symbol version, and wordmark — so your brand adapts to every application. Social profile pics, favicons, business cards, signage. All covered.

03

Color Palette

A curated set of primary, secondary, and neutral colors with exact HEX, RGB, and CMYK values. Every color chosen to position your brand and work beautifully across print and digital.

04

Typography System

A heading font and a body font — selected to complement each other and your brand personality. With sizing guidance and hierarchy rules so every piece of text feels intentional.

05

Brand Guidelines

A complete guide covering how to use your logo, colors, and type correctly — and what to avoid. So your brand stays consistent whether it's you, a printer, or a new hire using it.

06

All File Formats

SVG, PNG, PDF, EPS — every format for every use case. Web, print, embroidery, signage. You walk away with a complete asset library, organized and ready to use immediately.

Why it matters

Your brand is the first thing people judge.
Make it impossible to ignore.

First impressions happen in 0.05 seconds

That's how long it takes someone to form an opinion about your brand. A weak identity loses clients before you ever get the chance to speak. A strong one earns trust before you open your mouth.

Consistency builds trust

Brands that look the same everywhere — website, social, cards, packaging — feel more legitimate. People buy from businesses they trust, and trust starts with how you look.

Your identity is an asset, not an expense

A great brand identity pays for itself over and over. It attracts better clients, justifies higher prices, and makes everything you put out look more credible. It's the best investment your business can make.
